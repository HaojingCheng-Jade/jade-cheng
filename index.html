About Me 😃

When a Netflix Show Sparked a Career Path

My data science journey started with an unexpected mentor: the TV series House of Cards. I was captivated when I discovered how Netflix used millions of user interactions—from pauses and searches to ratings—to shape everything from casting choices to plot direction. It was my "aha!" moment: raw data could tell compelling stories and drive powerful business strategies.

That revelation led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Data Science at Communication University of China, where I learned that data science isn't just about algorithms—it's about finding the story hidden in the numbers.

Building Skills That Turn Data into Decisions

I believe the best way to learn is by doing. That's why I'm always getting my hands dirty with data:

  • At China Mobile, I developed interactive dashboards using Python and Power BI to transform base station data into actionable insights about national travel patterns, directly informing regional marketing strategies
  • During my ICBC internship, I took deep dives into product-focused data analysis—writing SQL queries to extract user behavior data, designing and analyzing A/B tests to evaluate feature performance, and translating user insights into actionable product recommendations
  • I architected an intelligent news recommendation system leveraging large language models and the Flask framework, implementing machine learning models to deliver personalized content

Now, as a first-year Master's student in Statistical Science at Duke University, I'm expanding my toolkit with Python (NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn), SQL, A/B Testing, Machine Learning, and LLM APIs—all while keeping my eyes on the prize: making data work in the real world.

Let's Build Something Meaningful Together!!!😆

I'm excited to find a Summer 2026 internship where I can contribute my skills, learn from experienced mentors, and help turn complex data into clear, actionable insights. Because at the end of the day, it's not just about the models we build—it's about the impact they create.
